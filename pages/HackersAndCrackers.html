Hackers and Crackers

Data has a value. The more accurate and complete the data, the more valuable it is. Computer security protects this valuable data from theft or corruption.

Data can be corrupted in a number of ways:

  • Human error
  • Theft
  • Physical causes
  • Power failure
  • Hardware failure
  • Misplacing portable media

Malware is designed to disrupt or modify a computer system and its data. It is installed on a computer system without the user's knowledge.


Phishing is when you trick a user into giving away personal data that they should not (credit card number, etc.)


Pharming is when you trick a user by recreating a trusted website and manipulating them into handing over their credit card details on the website.


DoS attacks are when you deny a service to a user. A DDoS is when this attack is distributed.
